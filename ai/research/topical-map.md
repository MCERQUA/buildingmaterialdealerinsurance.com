1. **Dealer specificity** — most "contractor insurance" sites ignore dealers entirely. We name the buyer (lumber yard, building supply, hardware distributor) and the exact exposures.
2. **Fire + inventory depth** — lumber stacks are a catastrophic fire exposure; we own property/inventory/fire coverage deeply.
3. **Product liability gap** — dealers sell goods made by others; the product-liability exposure is unique and widely misunderstood. We explain it correctly.
4. **Forklift + yard operations** — we name the actual hazards (forklifts, loading docks, contractor customers on premises).
5. **Delivery fleet reality** — most dealers run box/flatbed trucks. We cover commercial auto + inland marine together.
6. **Former-contractor credibility** — Josh Cotner's 20-year, all-50-state, ex-contractor background.
